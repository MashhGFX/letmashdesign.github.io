About Me

Hello! I'm Mashh, a third-year Computer Science student with a profound passion for bringing ideas to life through dynamic visual design.

My journey into graphic design began with a deep dive into the world of digital creativity, constantly pushing boundaries and refining my craft. I specialize in creating engaging visuals that truly stand out, with a particular focus on the high-octane world of gaming and YouTube content.

  • Eye-catching Gaming Thumbnails: Designed to maximize click-through rates and capture viewer attention.
  • Impactful Posters: Crafting compelling visual narratives for events, brands, and personal projects.
  • Seamless YouTube Live Stream Overlays: Enhancing the professional look and feel of live broadcasts.
  • Comprehensive YouTube & Streaming Graphics: From channel art to end screens, and all necessary branding elements for streamers.

I'm continuously learning and exploring new design techniques to deliver fresh, innovative, and effective visual solutions. Every project is an opportunity to combine technical precision with creative flair, ensuring your message is not just seen, but remembered.

Let's collaborate and create something visually spectacular! Feel free to get in touch to discuss your next project.
